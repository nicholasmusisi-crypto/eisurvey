Screen Monitoring Consent Notice
Please read carefully before proceeding
Why We Monitor

This assessment is designed to evaluate your individual knowledge, skills, and independent thinking. To ensure the results are fair and genuinely reflect your own capabilities, we monitor screen activity during the session. Monitoring protects the integrity of the assessment for all participants.

N.B The monitoring process doesn't stop you from exploring any tools at your disposal. We just need to know what was used.

What Will Be Captured
  • Your screen content via browser screen sharing
  • Tab switches and application changes, with timestamps
  • Significant changes in screen content (e.g. opening a browser or new application)
  • Screenshot images captured at the moment a change is flagged
  • A timestamped log of all detected events during the session
How Reports Are Stored & Used
  • All monitoring data is processed locally in your browser during the session — nothing is uploaded in real time
  • A text summary report is automatically sent to your assessor when you end the session
  • A PDF report, which may include screenshots, is downloaded and retained by the assessor for review
  • This data is used solely to verify assessment integrity and will not be shared with third parties
  • Reports are retained only for as long as necessary to support the assessment process
